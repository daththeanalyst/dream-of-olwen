Featured Pieces

Scarves

Sanganeri & Banarasi Scarves

Hand block-printed cottons from Sanganer, shimmering Banarasi silks with zari thread, and earthy Bagru dabu prints from Rajasthan.

Pashminas

Kashmiri Pashminas

Hand-woven from Changthangi goat wool in Kashmir. Plain, Aari chain-stitch embroidered, or Sozni needle-worked — each a work of art.

Bed Linen

Block-Printed Bed Sheets

Sanganeri and Bagru hand block-printed cotton bedding in floral and paisley motifs. Natural vegetable dyes in traditional and contemporary designs.

Cushion Covers

Shisha, Kantha & Brocade Cushions

Gujarati mirror-work, Bengali Kantha stitch and Banarasi silk brocade — handcrafted cushion covers that bring Indian artistry into your home.

Find Us

In the Heart of Bath

We're the first stall you'll see as you enter Bath's historic Guildhall Market from the High Street. Just 80 yards from Bath Abbey, our colourful display of textiles has been welcoming visitors for over two decades.

Whether you're a Bath local or a visitor exploring this beautiful UNESCO World Heritage city, drop in and discover something special.
