Getting Here

By Train Bath Spa station is a 10-minute walk. Head up Manvers Street towards the city centre.
By Bus Most city buses stop at Bath Bus Station or on Dorchester Street, both within 5 minutes' walk.
By Car Nearest car parks: Charlotte Street (3 min walk), Southgate (5 min walk). Note: the city centre is compact — walking is often easiest.

About Bath Guildhall Market

One of England's oldest covered markets, trading on this site for over 800 years. Home to around 20 unique independent stalls in the heart of Bath.

◆

800 Years of Trading

The Guildhall Market has been a fixture of Bath life since the medieval era — one of England's most historic markets.
