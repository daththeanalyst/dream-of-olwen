Our Story

A passion for colour, craft and Indian textiles

Our Journey

Over 20 Years of Beautiful Textiles

Dream of Olwen has been a cherished part of Bath's Guildhall Market for over two decades. What started as a passion for the rich colours and extraordinary craftsmanship of Indian textiles has grown into one of the market's most distinctive stalls.

We travel regularly to India, visiting artisan workshops and cooperatives to hand-select every piece in our collection. From the silk weavers of Varanasi to the block printers of Rajasthan, each item carries the skill and tradition of generations of craftspeople.

Our Values

Direct from Artisans to Bath

We believe in fair, direct trade with the craftspeople who create our textiles. By buying directly from workshops and cooperatives, we ensure that artisans receive fair payment for their extraordinary skill.

Every scarf, pashmina, bed sheet and cushion cover in our collection has been personally selected for its quality, beauty and authenticity. We don't do mass-produced — each piece is unique.
