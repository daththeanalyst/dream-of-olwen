Our Collection

Handpicked textiles from the finest artisans across India

Category

Scarves

From lightweight silk blends for summer to warm wool wraps for winter — each scarf tells a story of Indian craftsmanship.

Block-Print

Sanganeri Block-Print Scarf

Hand block-printed in Sanganer, near Jaipur. Artisans carve intricate floral motifs — rose, lotus, marigold — onto wooden blocks, then stamp each colour individually. Natural vegetable dyes on pure cotton. A craft dating back to the 16th century.

Silk

Banarasi Silk Scarf

Woven in Varanasi using traditional handloom techniques passed down through generations. Known for gold and silver brocade (zari) work, each scarf shimmers with delicate metallic thread detailing.

Mud-Resist Print

Bagru Dabu Print Scarf

Made using the ancient dabu mud-resist printing technique from Bagru, Rajasthan. Eco-friendly natural dyes — turmeric for yellow, indigo for blue, madder for red — create earthy geometric motifs unique to each piece.

Category

Pashminas

Luxurious, soft and warm — our pashminas are hand-woven from the finest Kashmiri fibres.

Kashmiri

Kashmiri Pashmina

Made from the wool of Himalayan Changthangi goats — fibres just 12-16 microns in diameter, finer than regular cashmere. Hand-combed from the goats in summer and hand-woven on traditional looms in Kashmir. Incredibly soft, lightweight, yet wonderfully warm.

Aari Embroidery

Aari Embroidered Pashmina

Traditional Kashmiri needlework using fine chain stitches. Motifs of chinar leaves, paisleys and florals — each shawl takes weeks to months of painstaking hand-embroidery. Perfect for weddings, theatre nights and special occasions.

Sozni Embroidery

Sozni Embroidered Pashmina

The finest Kashmiri embroidery technique. Needle-worked on both sides so the shawl is fully reversible — equally beautiful from either face. Extremely labour-intensive; a single shawl can take months of dedicated work.

Category

Bed Linen

Transform your bedroom with hand-printed Indian cotton bedding in traditional and contemporary designs.

Sanganeri Print

Sanganeri Block-Print Bed Sheet

Hand block-printed in Sanganer using traditional wooden blocks. Floral and paisley motifs in natural vegetable dyes on pure cotton. Available in single, double and king sizes.

Bagru Print

Bagru Hand-Printed Bedding Set

Matching sheet, pillowcases and throw in Rajasthan's distinctive Bagru dabu print. Earthy, muted tones from natural dyes — each set coordinates beautifully whilst retaining the hand-printed character of each piece.

Category

Cushion Covers

Add a splash of Indian artistry to any room with our intricately embroidered and printed cushion covers.

Shisha Embroidery

Gujarati Mirror-Work (Shisha) Cushion

Small mirrors hand-stitched into vibrant colourful embroidery — a tradition of the Kutch region of Gujarat. Each piece is made by tribal artisan women, with patterns passed down through generations. A dazzling centrepiece for any room.

Kantha Stitch

Kantha Stitch Cushion Cover

Traditional Bengali running-stitch technique where layers of fabric are stitched together with decorative patterns. Originally used to repurpose old saris, now a celebrated art form. Soft, textured and full of character.

Banarasi Silk

Banarasi Silk Brocade Cushion

Luxurious silk brocade woven in Varanasi with gold zari thread detailing. The same ancient weaving tradition used for bridal saris, applied to create opulent cushion covers for your home.
